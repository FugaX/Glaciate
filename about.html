What is Trayce?

Trayce is a dialect of HTML designed to simplify the web dev process. Not only does Trayce make it easier to make applets and websites, but it is also implementable in other libraries like Bootstrap, React, angular, etc.)

Why Trayce?

Trayce was made in order to not only help people learn the ropes of programming, but it was also made in order to provide a great alternative towards app and webdevelopment since there are quite alot of people who have great app ideas, but are not sure how to make them a reality.
